About Reams Family Creations

We’re a family shop that believes in making things by hand — with heart and with an eye for lasting quality. Every piece we make is designed for legacy: to be used, enjoyed, and passed down.

From custom tables and cutting boards to key holders and wall art, we work in solid wood and pay attention to grain, joinery, and finish. Whether you have a clear vision or just a rough idea, we’re here to turn it into something you’ll love.

We don’t do mass production. We do one-off and small-batch work, so each item gets the time and care it deserves. When you order from us, you’re not just buying a product — you’re supporting a family craft and taking home something made with intention.

How our CNC inlay is different

A lot of “personalized” boards you see online are laser engraved—the design is burned into the surface. We often work the other way: a design is routed as a pocket, a second piece of wood is cut to fit that pocket flush, then the whole panel is planed smooth and finished with a durable, food-safe topcoat when it’s a cutting board.

Typical steps:

  1. Layout the pattern and choose contrasting woods.
  2. CNC cut the recess (pocket) for the design.
  3. Inlay the matching piece, glue, clamp, and plane or sand flat.
  4. Add juice grooves or edges as needed, then seal for use.

That’s real thickness and grain in the motif—not just a darkened surface—which is why the work catches the light differently and holds up like the rest of the board.
